The war is ended by the mass deportation of over 20000 Estonians to inhospitable areas of the Soviet Union. 1960 - Some of the deported Estonians are allowed to return to the country. 1987 - The first open protests against Soviet rule occur on the streets of Estonian cities. 2017 - NATO sends 800 British troops to Estonia in an effort to combat what it sees as Russian aggression.
